When assessing cattle health, welfare, or reproductive status:

**WELFARE ASSESSMENT (Fat Score 0 subcategories):**

High Risk 1 (HR1):
- Muscle depletion at back/loin/hindleg, concave rump
- Spines individually visible, ribs prominent and sharp
- Tailhead/pins hollow, udder shrinking
- Tail bones just identifiable, skin less pliable
- Mobile but less energetic, normal dung/cud
- TRANSPORT: To abattoir/agistment only, unsuitable for long distances

High Risk 2 (HR2):
- Emaciated, hooks/pins/ribs individually identifiable
- Inside pins deeply sunken to bone, stifle joint visible
- Deeply concave rump, udder shrunk/tucked up
- Dewlap is skin fold, sternum visible
- Spines pointed to touch, skin tight, loose hump skin (Bos indicus)
- Dung shows poor rumen function (undigested feed/mucous/watery)
- TRANSPORT: Unable to transport without prolonged intensive management

Downer:
- All HR2 signs PLUS: unsteady gait/cannot stand, "plaiting" hind legs
- Eyes tearing/sunken/glazed, paddle marks from feet
- Immobile, no response to stimuli
- ACTION REQUIRED: Seek veterinary advice for euthanasia immediately

**Lameness Score (1-5):**
1: Normal gait, flat back, steady head
2: Arched back while walking, slight limp, keeps up with mob
3: Short-striding, arched back standing/walking, obvious affected limb
4: Reluctant weight-bearing, hesitant strides, head bobbing (NOT FIT TO LOAD)
5: Extreme reluctance, severe restriction, must be encouraged to move (NOT FIT TO LOAD)

**Pregnancy Status:**
- Not Pregnant (NP): No pregnancy detected or <8 weeks, not mated
- Not Detectably Pregnant (NDP): No pregnancy >5 weeks detected, normal tract (PREgCHECK green/white tag)
- PTIC: Pregnancy confirmed (PREgCHECK blue/orange <4mo, red/yellow >4mo)
- Unjoined: Not known to be mated
- Joined: Run with bulls or AI'd

Require signed Statement of Pregnancy for trade (state regulations apply).

**Calving Ease (1-6 scale):**
1: Unassisted/no difficulty
2: Easy pull (1 person, no mechanical)
3: Hard pull (2 people or 1+mechanical)
4: Surgical/vet intervention
5: Abnormal presentation (breech)
6: Elective surgical (before cow attempts)

**Treatments Recording (MANDATORY):**
Record: Date, product name/number, dose, batch, expiry, WHPs/ESIs
- WHP (Withholding Period): Minimum days before domestic slaughter
- ESI (Export Slaughter Interval): Minimum days before export slaughter
Must document on NVD. Check APVMA register for current ESI/WHP.

**Coat/Hide Conditions:**
- Coat Score 1-7 (1=very short/sleek, 7=very hairy)
- Dag Score 0-5 (5=extensive, notify buyer)
- Tick Score 0-5 (>4.5mm ticks only)
- Buffalo Fly Lesions 1-5
- Ringworm 0-5
Document if market requires.
