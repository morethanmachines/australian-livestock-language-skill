- 12mo: Score 5 = 124cm (steers) / 130cm (bulls)
- 18mo: Score 5 = 132cm (steers) / 138cm (bulls)  
- 24mo: Score 5 = 137cm (steers) / 142cm (bulls)

Heifers - Select scores for common ages:
- 12mo: Score 5 = 119cm
- 18mo: Score 5 = 126cm

**DRESSING PERCENTAGE ESTIMATES (HSCW ÷ Live Weight × 100):**

Base estimates (≈2hr off pasture):
- Yearlings Fat 2-3: 50-55%
- Steers Fat 2-3: 51-54%, Fat 4: 54-57%
- Heifers Fat 2-3: 50-53%, Fat 4: 53-56%
- Cows <200kg Fat 2-3: 39-42%, Fat 4: 43-48%
- Cows 200-250kg Fat 2-3: 42-46%, Fat 4: 46-50%
- Cows >250kg Fat 2-3: 43-48%, Fat 4: 48-52%
- Bulls <200kg: 48-54%, 200-300kg: 53-57%, >300kg: 54-58%

Curfew adjustments (add to base %):
- 1hr off feed: +0.75%
- 2hr: +1.25%
- 4hr: +2.0%
- 12hr: +3.5%
- 16hr: +4.0%

**WEIGHT LOSS DURING CURFEW (% of live weight):**

**SEX CLASSIFICATION WITH DENTITION (AUS-MEAT ciphers):**
Basic categories:
- Veal *V*: 0 teeth, ≤150kg HSCW, no SSC
- Beef *A*: 0-8 teeth, no SSC (any sex)
- Bull *B*: 0-8 teeth, shows SSC (entire or castrate)

Alternative ciphers (most common):
- Yearling Steer *YS*: 0 teeth, no SSC (castrate/entire male)
- Yearling Beef *Y*: 0 teeth, no SSC (any sex)
- Young Steer *YGS*: 0-2 teeth, no SSC (castrate/entire male)
- Young Beef *YG*: 0-2 teeth, no SSC (any sex)
- Young Prime Steer *YPS*: 0-4 teeth, no SSC (castrate/entire male)
- Young Prime Beef *YP*: 0-4 teeth, no SSC (any sex)
- Prime Steer *PRS*: 0-7 teeth, no SSC (castrate/entire male)
- Prime Beef *PR*: 0-7 teeth, no SSC (any sex)
- Steer *SS*: 0-8 teeth, no SSC (castrate/entire male)
- Ox *S*: 0-7 teeth (female only)
- Cow *C*: 8 teeth (female)

**SCROTAL CIRCUMFERENCE STANDARDS (365-day adjusted, cm):**
Varies by breed - consult breed society for specific standards.
Typical acceptable ranges at 365 days: 30-38cm.
Formula: Actual SC (cm²) × [Age adjustment factor × (365 - Actual age in days)]

**CALVING EASE SCORES:**
1=Unassisted, 2=Easy pull (1 person), 3=Hard pull (2 people/mechanical), 4=Surgical, 5=Abnormal presentation, 6=Elective surgical

**TEMPERAMENT SCORES:**
1=Docile (low risk), 2=Restless (medium), 3=Nervous (medium), 4=Flighty (high risk), 5=Aggressive (high risk)

**COAT SCORES:**
1=Extremely short (sleek), 2=Very short, 3=Fairly short, 4=Fairly long, 5=Long, 6=Hairy, 7=Very hairy

**DAG/MUD CONTAMINATION SCORES:**
0=Clean, 1=Fresh manure/mud legs only, 2=Fresh manure/mud below elbow-stifle line, 3=Below stifle-shoulder line, 4=Adhered dags below stifle-shoulder, 5=Extensive dags on ribs/underline/legs (notify buyer)

**LAMENESS SCORES:**
1=Normal, 2=Mildly lame (slight limp), 3=Moderately lame (short stride), 4=Lame (NOT FIT TO LOAD), 5=Severely lame (NOT FIT TO LOAD)

**KEY MEASUREMENT SITES:**
- P8: Intersection of line from 3rd sacral vertebra crest + line parallel to backbone from pin bone
- Rib fat: Between 12th-13th rib (most common), or 10th-11th, 11th-12th
- EMA: M. longissimus dorsi at ribbing site
- Hump height: From ligamentum nuchae to highest hump point (5mm increments)
